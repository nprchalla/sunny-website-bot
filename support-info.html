Support & Information

Learn how Sunny Reddy AI is designed, how to get the most value from it, and how it connects to Sunny’s real-world work in education, entrepreneurship, and community-building.

What Sunny Reddy AI Is (and Is Not)

  • Sunny Reddy AI is a digital companion inspired by Sunny Reddy’s values, life experience, and commitment to leadership, education, and community. It’s designed to help you think, reflect, and communicate more clearly by offering ideas, perspectives, and thoughtful next steps based on general knowledge and what you choose to share. This is a space for exploration and growth, not a substitute for professional advice.
  • It is: a place to practice communication, explore leadership choices, and reflect on community impact.
  • It is not: a replacement for professional legal, medical, financial, or mental health guidance.
  • Your judgment matters: always pair AI insights with your own experience, values, and guidance from people you trust.

How to Use Sunny Reddy AI Effectively

  • Be specific about your situation, goals, and constraints.
  • Ask follow-up questions to explore different options and perspectives.
  • Use the AI to rehearse important conversations or decisions before you act.
  • Write down the actions you want to take after each session.

You can access Sunny Reddy AI through ChatGPT in any browser. For live conversation, click the Voice button inside ChatGPT once the coach is open.
